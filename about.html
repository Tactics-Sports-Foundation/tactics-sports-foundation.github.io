About Us

Tactics Sports Foundation is a non-profit organization. It is an officially recognized sports promotion and sports development organization in Tamil Nadu. It was founded in 2021 by Lakshmanan, Raman, Rajkumar, Naveenkumar and Vignesh. By establishing this foundation we are fulfilling our long-held dreams. How about we had great interest and talent in sports during our school days, but due to a lack of proper guidance and coaching, we did not get opportunities to showcase our talents. So through this foundation, we aim to provide equal opportunities to those who have great passion and talent in sports like us without any discrimination.

Our goal at the Tactics Sports Foundation is to create positive environments, provide education, and offer opportunities for underprivileged children through sports.

Our foundation helps to create awareness about sports, to identify the students who are interested in sports and provide suitable training and send them to national and international level competitions, also for posting to government jobs available through the game and send them to SDAT, SAI Sports Hostel.

Additionally, our foundation organizes camps, district-level tournaments, state-level tournaments, seminars and village awareness meetings on sports to further reach our mission.

MISSION

Our mission is to harness the power of sports to inspire and empower individuals, especially the underprivileged, to attain personal and collective excellence. We are dedicated to establishing sustainable programs that foster physical activity, advocate for healthy lifestyles, and cultivate essential life skills like teamwork, leadership, and resilience. Through our initiatives, we aim to address disparities in sports access and create a nurturing environment for overall development.

VISION

Our vision is to create a world where every individual has the opportunity to experience the joy and benefits of sports. We aim to build inclusive communities where sports serve as a platform for positive social change, empowering individuals to reach their full potential, and fostering unity, respect, and mutual understanding.

VALUES

Our values are the cornerstone of our mission and guide everything we do. We are committed to providing equal opportunities for all, regardless of age, gender, socio-economic background, or physical abilities, ensuring inclusivity in all our programs. Upholding the highest standards of honesty, transparency, and ethical behaviour, we act with integrity in all our actions. We strive for excellence, continually seeking to improve and innovate in our initiatives. Respect, empathy, and understanding are promoted both on and off the field, fostering a culture of mutual respect. We believe in the power of community, working collaboratively with partners, volunteers, and stakeholders to achieve our goals. Driven by a deep passion for sports, we harness its ability to transform lives and build stronger, healthier communities.
