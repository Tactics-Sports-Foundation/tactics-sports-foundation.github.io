Impact of Your Donation

Every donation to Tactics Sports Foundation helps us achieve meaningful outcomes. Here's an example of how your support has made a difference:

  • A donation helped provide sports equipment and coaching sessions to underprivileged youth in our community. Many of these children have now developed skills and confidence that extend beyond the playing field.
  • With your support, we organized a sports event for children with disabilities, promoting inclusivity and showcasing their talents to the community.
  • Funding from donors like you enabled us to renovate our sports facilities, creating a safe and welcoming environment for athletes of all ages.

Tax Information

Tactics Sports Foundation is a registered non-profit organization. Your donations may be tax-deductible under section 80G of the Income Tax Act. Please consult your tax advisor for details.

Donations to us are exempt from Indian Income Tax under section 80G of the IT Act. According to the new regulations, if you require an 80G exemption and need Form 10BE (the new 80G exemption certificate) from us, please fill out this form (Click here) with your PAN number and other required details without any errors. This will enable us to email you Form 10BE promptly, ensuring it reaches you before May 31 each year. If you have already submitted all necessary details, you will receive Form 10BE from us promptly.
