Pencak Silat Championship 2021 - 2022


The 2nd Tamil Nadu Inter District Pencak Silat Championship was held at Cheran College of Physical Education, Karur District from 26th to 27th February.

In this Competition, 38 Players from Dharmapuri participated on behalf of Dharmapuri through Our Foundation and won 13 Gold, 11 Silver, and 9 Bronze medals and bringing pride to the Dharmapuri district.
