1st Dharmapuri District Pencak Silat Championship 2021 - 2022


Dharmapuri District Pencak Silat Championship in collaboration with our Foundation and Dharmapuri District Pencak Silat Association was held at NXT Generation Badminton Club, Near Paramveer School, Papparapatti Main Road on 20-02-2022. Forty-Three players from the Dharmapuri District participated in this event.

Summer CAMP 2021 - 2022


Our Foundation and Dharmapuri District Pengak Silat Association organized Dharmapuri district level Pencak Silat and Silambam training camp from 15-05-2022 to 31-05-2022 at Bedarahalli School Campus and from 10-06-2022 to 20-06-2022 at Paparapatti School Campus. 60 players participated in this camp.

2nd Dharmapuri District Pencak Silat Championship 2022 - 2023


Dharmapuri District Pencak Silat Championship in collaboration with our Foundation and Dharmapuri District Pencak Silat Association was held at SM Arumugam Polytechnic College, Adhiyamaan Bye Pass Road, Dharmapuri on 03-10-2022. One Hundred and Seventy Eight players from the Dharmapuri District participated in this event.

3rd Dharmapuri District Pencak Silat Championship 2023 - 2024


Dharmapuri District Pencak Silat Championship in collaboration with our Foundation and Dharmapuri District Pencak Silat Association was held at P.U.M.School ,Thithiyoppanahalli, On 08- 10-2023. One Hundred and Twenty players from the Dharmapuri District participated in this event.
